iPhone 8

The iPhone 8 and the improved iPhone 8 Plus went on sale in 2017. The smartphones are equipped with a six-core A11 Bionic SoC processor with a tri-core graphics processor, supporting optical and digital zoom up to 6 times in video, and up to 10 times in photos.

iPhone X

The model was presented by the company in 2017 and was produced until the appearance of its updated version iPhone XS. The number X in the name indicates the decade of the entire iPhone line. The smartphone with a 5.8-inch Super Retina screen and a resolution of 2436x1125 is equipped with a 12-megapixel camera with optical stabilization.

iPhone XS

In the fall of 2018, Apple presented three models of Apple smartphones at once, among which were the iPhone XS and its improved version, the iPhone XS Max. The new generation had a version with 512 GB of storage.
